## Welcome to Quidkey

Quidkey enables merchants to accept **bank-to-bank payments** directly in their checkout, reducing payment costs and eliminating chargeback risk. Integrate once and offer customers a seamless alternative to card payments.

## Why Use Quidkey?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
<Card title="Lower Fees" icon="chart-line-down">

</Card>

<Card title="No Chargebacks" icon="shield-check">

<Card title="Seamless UX" icon="sparkles">

<Note>
Quidkey works alongside Stripe - customers can choose between card payments or bank transfers. Quidkey automatically predicts and pre-selects the customer's bank, so they pay with their trusted bank brand. Payment amounts and rewards can be updated dynamically for shipping costs, discounts, and promotional offers.
</Note>

## How It Works

<Steps>
<Step title="Quidkey predicts customer's bank">
  Quidkey automatically predicts and pre-selects the customer's bank
</Step>

<Step title="Customer pays with their bank">
  Customer sees their trusted bank pre-selected and ready to pay
</Step>

<Step title="Authenticate & approve">
  Customer is redirected into their bank to authorizes the payment securely
</Step>

<Step title="Payment complete">
  Funds transfer directly to your account - you receive a webhook with payment confirmation
</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Quidkey connects to multiple Open Banking providers (TPPs) and bank integration partners. We automatically select the best connection for each transaction to ensure high success rates and a smooth payment experience.
</Info>
