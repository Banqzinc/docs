## Welcome to Quidkey

Quidkey enables merchants to accept **bank-to-bank payments** directly in their checkout, reducing payment costs and eliminating chargeback risk. Integrate once and offer customers a seamless alternative to card payments.

## Why Use Quidkey?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
<Card title="Lower Fees" icon="chart-line-down">

</Card>

<Card title="No Chargebacks" icon="shield-check">

<Card title="Seamless UX" icon="sparkles">

<Note>
Quidkey works alongside Stripe - customers choose their preferred payment method at checkout. Payment amounts and rewards can be updated dynamically for shipping costs, discounts, and promotional offers.
</Note>

## How It Works

<Steps>
<Step title="Customer chooses Quidkey">
  At checkout, customers see Quidkey as a payment option alongside credit cards
</Step>

<Step title="Select their bank">
  Customer picks their bank from a list (or we predict it for them)
</Step>

<Step title="Authenticate & approve">
  Customer logs into their bank and authorizes the payment securely
</Step>

<Step title="Payment complete">
  Funds transfer directly to your account - you receive a webhook with payment confirmation
</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Quidkey uses **Open Banking** technology - the same secure infrastructure powering millions of transactions across Europe and beyond.
</Info>
